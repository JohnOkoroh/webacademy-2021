The benefits of rehabilitation:

Rehab offers a multitude of therapies and treatments to help with all the different parts of addiction. Addiction is a physical, mental, and psychological disease, and should be treated as such. Therapy will help to uncover any triggers that set off the desire to use substances. It can help the individual understand the flaws in their thinking and in their behaviors that lead to bad decision-making.
With this, therapy can help individuals make productive and healthier thoughts, as well as learn new healthy coping mechanisms to prevent relapses.
